Awakening the Best of the Human Spirit.

Greenshire’s Space for Peace is dedicated to helping people explore new horizons as they revitalize the quality of life: body, mind, and spirit. We invite you to explore our many gatherings and workshops such as Yoga, Reiki, Meditation, Nutrition, Whole-Body Health, Spiritual Studies, Astrology, Drumming, Fire Circles, Gardening, Retreats, and Community Gatherings.  

Greenshire is located in Quakertown, PA, near Lake Nockamixon. 
We remain open to people of all ages, and ethnic, religious, political, and cultural backgrounds.
